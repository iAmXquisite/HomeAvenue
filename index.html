What we do

Home Avenue is an Adult Day Health Care (ADHC) and Community-Based Adult Services (CBAS) Program that serves individuals 18 and over with chronic medical and/or mental impairments. Home Avenue ADHC/CBAS promote each of our Participants’ self-care, optimal mental/physical health, independence, self-determination in assisting them to “Live Life to the Fullest”.

Our Goal

Home Avenue is committed to providing you with high quality health care. Our friendly skilled staff of health care professionals are here to help you achieve optimum health so you can “live life to the fullest”.

Services

Basic Services at Home Avenue include: Individual Needs Assessment, Nursing Care, Rehabilitative Therapies, Social Services, Recreational Activities, Transportation, Meals and Snacks, Liaison with Participants’ Physicians and Family/Caregivers.
